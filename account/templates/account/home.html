About AIIMS

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) was established in 1956 with the goal of developing a pattern of teaching in postgraduate and undergraduate medical education in all its branches so that it can attain a high standard of medical education in India. AIIMS is a pioneer institution in medical research, and its comprehensive healthcare services are accessible to a large population.
