All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS)

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), located in New Delhi, is a premier medical college and research institute established by an Act of Parliament in 1956. It is known for its high standards in medical education, healthcare, and research.

AIIMS offers a range of medical services and specialties, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, and more. It has a team of highly skilled healthcare professionals dedicated to providing quality patient care and conducting advanced medical research.

The institute is recognized for its innovative healthcare delivery system, commitment to patient care, and contributions to medical education and research in India. AIIMS also serves as a model for other medical institutions in the country, emphasizing the importance of quality healthcare accessible to all.

For more information, visit their official website: AIIMS.
